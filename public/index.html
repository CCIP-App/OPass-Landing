OPass

OPass 是整合活動資訊的解決方案，報到、活動時程、大會公告…等第一手資訊盡在 OPass！

OPass 原本是是為了台灣開源資訊社群研討會所開發的報到 App，在 SITCON 學生計算機年會及 COSCUP 開源人年會已多次使用，為了各活動的使用需求便啟動了整合計畫，成為了 OPass 大平台來繼續提供給大家最好的報到體驗，並提供第一手的年會資訊。

也歡迎您一同加入貢獻 https://github.com/CCIP-App/

OPass provides the newest event information for you. Use OPass to check in, plan your schedule and get event announcement!

OPass is Taiwan's FLOSS conference check-in solution. It has been used in SITCON (Students' Information Technology Conference) & COSCUP (Conference for Open Source Coders, Users and Promoters) for so many years. This app will help you check in rapidly and provide the newest information about the conference.

Patch welcome! https://github.com/CCIP-App/
