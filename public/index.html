OPass

OPass 是整合活動資訊的解決方案，報到、活動時程、大會公告…等第一手資訊盡在 OPass！

OPass 是為了台灣開源資訊社群研討會所開發的報到 App，在 SITCON 學生計算機年會及 COSCUP 開源人年會已多次使用，為了各活動的使用需求便啟動了整合計畫成為了 OPass 來繼續提供給大家最好的報到體驗，並提供第一手的年會資訊。

也歡迎您一同加入貢獻 https://github.com/CCIP-App/

OPass provide newest event information for you, use OPass to checkin, plan your schedule and get event announcement!

OPass is Taiwan's FLOSS conference check-in solution. It's use in SITCON (Students' Information Technology Conference) & COSCUP (Conference for Open Source Coders, Users and Promoters) so many years, this app will help you check-in rapidly and provide newest information about the conference.

Patch welcome! https://github.com/CCIP-App/
